Access Door and Interlocking System

Trusted Access Door and Interlocking System Manufacturers in India

Secure and controlled access is essential for modern industrial and commercial facilities. As trusted Access Door and Interlocking System Manufacturers in India, Samar Automation Pvt Ltd provides advanced access door solutions for cleanrooms, pharmaceutical units, hospitals, laboratories, commercial buildings, and industrial facilities that require safety, hygiene, and controlled entry systems.

Our range includes access doors, cleanroom interlocking doors, airlock door systems, electronic door interlock systems, and industrial access control door systems designed to improve security, maintain contamination control, and support smooth personnel and material movement. Whether you need a system for a pharmaceutical plant, research lab, hospital, or manufacturing unit, our solutions are built for reliable performance, durability, and long service life.

We understand the operational challenges faced by controlled environments, including contamination risk, unauthorized access, and pressure-sensitive areas. That is why our door systems are engineered to support safe movement, better environmental control, and efficient facility access.
